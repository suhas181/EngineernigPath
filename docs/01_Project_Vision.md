# 1. Project Overview

Engineering Path is an AI-powered career guidance platform designed specifically for engineering students.

The platform helps students identify their career goals, understand the skills they need, follow personalized learning roadmaps, build strong resumes, prepare for interviews, discover internships, participate in hackathons, and ultimately secure placements.

Instead of students searching across dozens of websites, Engineering Path brings everything together in one platform.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Most engineering students struggle because they don't know:

- What to learn
- In what order to learn
- Which resources are actually good
- Which projects to build
- How to make their resume stand out
- How to prepare for interviews
- Where to find internships
- Which companies they are ready for

Students waste months watching random YouTube videos without following a structured roadmap.

Many students from Tier-2 and Tier-3 colleges have little guidance.

# 11. Core Platform Features

Engineering Path aims to provide an all-in-one platform for engineering students with the following core features:

### 🎯 Personalized Career Roadmaps
- AI-generated learning paths based on career goals.
- Step-by-step guidance from beginner to placement.

### 📚 Curated Learning Resources
- High-quality free and paid learning resources.
- Resources filtered by difficulty and technology.

### 📄 AI Resume Analyzer
- Upload resume and receive an ATS score.
- AI suggestions for improving projects, skills, keywords, and formatting.

### 💼 Internship & Job Opportunities
- Browse verified internships and job openings.
- Filter by role, location, company, and eligibility.

### 🔔 Smart Internship Alerts
Students can set their career preferences such as:
- Preferred role (SDE, Frontend, Backend, ML, Data Analyst, etc.)
- Preferred companies
- Remote or on-site jobs
- Graduation year

Engineering Path will notify students whenever a matching internship or job is available through:
- In-app notifications
- Email notifications
- Future support for mobile push notifications

### 🧠 AI Skill Gap Analysis
- Compare student skills with industry requirements.
- Identify missing skills and recommend learning resources.

### 📈 Progress Tracking
- Track completed topics, projects, DSA problems, certifications, and overall learning progress.

### 🎤 Interview Preparation
- Technical interview preparation.
- HR interview questions.
- AI-powered mock interviews.

### 🏆 Career Opportunities
- Hackathons
- Coding contests
- Open-source programs
- Scholarships
- Workshops
- Bootcamps

### 🤖 AI Career Mentor
An AI assistant that helps students:
- Plan their learning journey.
- Recommend projects.
- Suggest interview preparation strategies.
- Answer career-related questions.
