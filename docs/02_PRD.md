# EngineerPath

---

# 1. Product Overview

EngineerPath is an AI-powered career guidance platform that helps engineering students navigate their journey from college to placement.

The platform provides personalized learning roadmaps, internship opportunities, resume analysis, interview preparation, AI mentorship, and progress tracking—all in one place.

Instead of using multiple websites for learning, internships, coding practice, resume building, and career planning, students can manage their complete career journey through EngineerPath.

---

# 2. Objectives

The primary objectives of EngineerPath are:

- Help students learn in the correct order.
- Reduce confusion while preparing for placements.
- Increase internship opportunities.
- Improve resume quality.
- Recommend personalized learning paths.
- Track student progress.
- Provide AI-powered career guidance.
- Keep students updated with internships and career opportunities.

---

# 4. Problem Statement

Engineering students face several challenges:

- Too many learning resources.
- No structured roadmap.
- Poor resume quality.
- Lack of mentorship.
- Difficulty finding internships.
- No centralized platform.
- Low confidence during placements.

EngineerPath solves these problems through personalized AI guidance and structured learning.
